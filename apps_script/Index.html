Observed anchor and forecast paths

Observed anchors remain visible, while the forecast layer shows a faint Monte Carlo path ensemble, a forecast median, and a 10th-90th percentile band from the 2025 endpoint onward.

Model guardrails and source policy

Observed rows always override forecasts. The model is anchored to official national stage trajectories and is shown here with explicit uncertainty and reach-to-95 probabilities.

  • Model family: Monte Carlo first-order Markov state model on regional residuals around the official national stage trajectories
  • Constraint: diagnosis, treatment, and suppression forecasts remain anchored to official national annual year-end cascade values; observed regional values always override modeled paths
  • Display policy: solid observed anchors, faint sampled path lines, a dashed forecast median, and a shaded uncertainty band
